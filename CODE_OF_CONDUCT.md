# Code of Conduct

## Standard

Be precise, evidence-bound, and civil. Criticize claims, methods, artifacts, and statistics. Do not harass people. Do not expose private data. Do not use this repository to launder medical claims into credibility.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may close issues, reject PRs, or block participants who submit private data, credential leaks, abusive content, unverifiable medical claims, or repeated unfalsifiable assertions.

## Scope

This applies to issues, pull requests, discussions, security reports, and any project-controlled communication channel.
